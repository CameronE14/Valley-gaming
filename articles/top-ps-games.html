PlayStation Games You Have To Play!

Here's a list of my favourite PS exclusives.

5. The Last of Us

It's crazy to think how long ago this game came out and how much I still think about it. It's no doubt one of Naught Dogs finest games. This game is hands down the best title the PS3 produced.

4. God of War

This is a excellent example of a series rebooting itself and becoming one of the most talked about games of all time. Kratos turns from mega douche to super dad. This is a fantastic story about a father and sons journey across a mythical land to lay rest his wifes ashes and show the player how much Kratos and Santa Monica Studio has changed.

3. Ghost of Tsushima

Style, style and more style. This game is gorgeous, and until Ghost of Tsushima 2 comes out, if that ever happens, there's not going to be anything that matches the beauty of Tsushima Island

2. Returnal

I don't want to brag here, but I beat this game in 2 Deaths. Housemarque knocked this one out of the park. Right off the bat, the game throws quite a lot at you and expects you to die, frequently. If you push through the difficulty you'll have a rewarding experience and I can't wait to see what these guys do next.

1. Bloodborne

Give us a remaster! The PS4 give us some great titles and I imagine people thought God of War would top the list, but come on. Nothing gets better than Bloodborne. This fast paced offering from Hidetaka Miyazaki is one of the best Souls games to be release and I think every PS owner should give it a go, if only once.
