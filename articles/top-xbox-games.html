Some of The BEST Xbox Games

Check out some great Xbox exclusives (even though most of these are out for Windows)

5. Gears of War

The single player of GoW was co-op heaven, but I feel like the game really shined in Multiplayer. I would love to go back to those days of getting home from school, hoping to god the 360 hasn't chewed through another disc and spend the evening popping heads in Canals. I wasn't a massive fan of the sequels but that doesn't take away the joy I had with the first in the series.

4. Sea of Thieves

The game was pretty...basic lets say, when it was released, however if you were to check this out now you'd be blown away by the amount of content there is. There's nothing like taking out a Sloop, finding a Galley and blowing it to smithereens while making off with some poor souls loot. Also, I'll never forget coming across the Megalodon for the first time with a french guy whose boat I blew up hiding in my cargo hold cheering for my downfall.

3. Ori and the Blind Forest

Such a charming little game that deserves every form of praise it gets. If you're looking for a colorful Metroidvania this is your boy.

2. Jet Set Radio Future

It's a tragedy this game hasn't been remade in some shape or form. It was something special back in its hayday. It was a wacky game with lots of different characters to play where you grind and graffiti the city of Tokyo-to. Set in the far future of 2024 (I love the fact there's flying cars in 2024) where the music matches the crazy scenery. This game needs a reboot!

1. Halo 3

I think a lot of Halo fans will label Halo 3 as the pinnacle of Bungies Halo series, and for me its no exception. A close second would come to Reach but that first time loading into Halo 3 and experiencing that music paired with a fantastic campain really sticks in my mind.
