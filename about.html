Hi! I'm Cameron. Follow @CameroonEvans

I'm 31 and i've been playing video games since I was 2 years old.
Potching around with the Sega Mega Drive. My first memories of gaming come from Sonic the Hedgehog, and that sweet, sweet soundtrack. I wouldn't be surprised if that's where my love for music and music in video games comes from.
Moving on to the PSX era, and with games like Crash Bandicoot, Medievil and Abe I was hooked and then I discovered Final Fantasy 7.
FF7 changed my life. I was too young to realise any kind of meaning behind the game but it helped develop my reading skills and understand emotions such as empathy. After the discovery of FF, I was completely entranced with JRPGs and I quickly started to discover other massive hits such as Chrono Trigger and Breath of Fire. Today, JRPGs still hold a warm snuggly place in my heart, and when things get stressful or a little too much, I know I've got countless new stories to get lost in and even some familiar faces to keep me company.

Let's hit the skip button and plop ourselves in the present. Gaming has never been bigger and so too does my interest grow.
I find myself quite interested in the entire industry these days. I keep myself in the know with most things industry wide and I find myself fascinated with the machinations of greed in some of these companies.
Which leads me here, developing a passion project for gaming news and trying to give a voice to indi studios.
In my spare time, I'd love to interview indi game devs from the UK and share thier stories here for everyone to enjoy and between that, I'll be putting out mostly Top 10 lists of genres I love.
